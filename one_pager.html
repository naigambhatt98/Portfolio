Artifact Summary

Title: Secure and Private AI for Robotics

Problem: Organizations need a clear plan to deploy AI in robotics while protecting sensitive data and meeting privacy obligations.

Audience: Executive leaders, legal, and technical stakeholders evaluating AI adoption.

Outcome: A concise presentation that outlines a robotics data lifecycle, privacy safeguards, metrics and cadence for security, and a 30, 60, 90 day roadmap that includes the creation of an AI governance board and integration with NIST RMF.
