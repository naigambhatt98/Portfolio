About This Project

This project explores AI security and privacy frameworks for robotics systems. It presents a PowerPoint presentation that outlines a robotics data lifecycle, GDPR‑aligned safeguards, measurable security metrics, and a 30/60/90‑day roadmap for AI governance. The result demonstrates my ability to translate complex AI risks into actionable strategies for executive and legal teams.

Process

Mapped data flow vulnerabilities in robotics and aligned goals with legal, technical, and executive needs.

3) Validate & align

4) Deliver the story

Designed slide layouts emphasizing clarity, measurable goals, and immediate applicability for decision‑makers.
