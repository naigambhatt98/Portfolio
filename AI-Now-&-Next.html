Retail & E-commerce

What happened — Saks Global • AI-Personalized Homepages (Aug 14, 2025)

  • +7% revenue per visitor during pilot
  • ~+10% conversion lift in early cohort
  • Rollout completed; expansion planned across portfolio brands

Why it matters — Personalization is the storefront logic. Real-time ranking chooses layout and product order for each visitor, reducing reliance on broad campaigns.

Metrics to watch — Revenue/visitor, Add-to-cart rate, Return rate vs. control.

Outlook — Cross-brand signals (Saks, Neiman, Bergdorf) can compound performance if governance and attribution stay tight.

Days since announcement: 2

Energy & Utilities

What happened — Constellation + GridBeyond / GoodLeap + CT Green Bank • AI Demand Response in PJM & AI-powered VPP (Jul 31 & Aug 13, 2025)

  • Digital twins forecast peaks and automate sub-meter dispatch
  • Residential solar+storage aggregated for peak reduction
  • Participants paid while improving local reliability

Why it matters — AI turns flexible demand and home storage into dispatchable capacity. Facilities can price curtailment like a financial option rather than a manual scramble.

Metrics to watch — $/MWh earned in DR, Avoided demand charges, Event participation rate.

Outlook — Expect deeper ties with data-center curtailment deals and time-of-use incentives as enrollment scales.

Days since announcement: 16

Manufacturing & Automotive

What happened — Ford • AiTriz (video ML) & MAIVS (image QA) (Aug 11, 2025)

  • ~35 stations (AiTriz) and ~700 stations (MAIVS) in NA plants
  • Millimeter-level misalignment checks with in-station decisions
  • Defect libraries speed root-cause and recurrence prevention

Why it matters — Real-time vision QA catches issues upstream, cutting rework and warranty risk while improving first-time-through.

Metrics to watch — First-time-through, Defects per unit, Rework hours, Warranty claim rate.

Outlook — Next up: torque, sealant, connector checks for a unified AI QA fabric.

Days since announcement: 5
